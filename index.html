About HealthArc

Built to simplify your wellness journey

HealthArc is a smart health monitoring application designed to help users track and manage important health insights in one place. The application combines health tracking with intelligent assistance to provide a simple and effective wellness experience. Developed as a learning project by passionate Computer Science students, HealthArc's goal is to create simple and useful mobile applications that improve everyday life.

Key Features

Everything you need to
monitor your health

Comprehensive tools built into one seamless experience.

🔐
OTP Authentication

Secure login using one-time password verification to protect user accounts and personal health data.

👤
Profile Page

Allows users to manage their personal health profile and information conveniently.

🦶
Step Counter

Tracks daily steps using the device sensors to encourage physical activity and healthy habits.

😴
Sleep Monitor

Monitors sleep duration and sleeping patterns for better rest and recovery insight.

❤️
Heart Rate Monitor

Tracks heart rate to help users stay aware of their cardiovascular health and fitness levels.

🩸
Blood Pressure Monitor

Helps users monitor and record blood pressure readings for long-term cardiovascular awareness.

🫁
Blood Oxygen (SpO₂)

Tracks oxygen saturation levels for respiratory health monitoring and early detection of anomalies.

🤖
AI Assistant

Provides smart assistance inside the app to answer health questions, guide users through their data, and offer personalized wellness recommendations based on your tracked metrics.
