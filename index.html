About HealthArc

HealthArc is a smart health monitoring application designed to help users track and manage important health insights in one place. It combines health tracking with intelligent assistance to provide a simple and effective wellness experience.

Key Features

  • OTP Authentication – Secure login
  • Profile Management – Manage personal health data
  • Step Counter – Track daily activity
  • Sleep Monitor – Analyze sleep patterns
  • Heart Rate Monitor – Monitor cardiovascular health
  • Blood Pressure Monitor
  • SpO₂ Monitor
  • AI Assistant – Smart guidance
