Privacy Policy

HealthArc is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled. The app may request permissions to access device sensors, physical activity data, and services such as Health Connect or Google Fit in order to function correctly. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties; however, limited data may be shared with trusted services strictly required for app functionality. All collected data is handled with appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, and you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your information at any time by contacting us. You may also stop using the app at any time, which will discontinue further data collection. HealthArc may update this privacy policy periodically, and any changes will be reflected on this page.
