01 — Introduction

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to HealthArc ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the HealthArc mobile application.

HealthArc is a smart health monitoring application developed by Abhishek Saket and Ankush Mishra, Computer Science students at Vindhya Institute of Technology and Science, Satna. The application was created as a learning project to help users track health activities such as sleep, step count, heart rate, blood pressure, and blood oxygen levels.

By using the HealthArc application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application.

02 — Data We Collect

Personal Information We Collect

When you create a profile in HealthArc, we collect the following personal information to personalize and enhance your health monitoring experience:

Data Field Description Purpose
Full Name Your legal first and last name Profile Identification
Date of Birth Your complete date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY) Age & Health Calculations
Age Your current age in years Health Metric Calibration
Gender Your gender identity Health Norm Calibration
Height Your height in cm or feet/inches BMI & Fitness Tracking
Weight Your weight in kg or lbs BMI & Fitness Tracking
Blood Group Your ABO/Rh blood type Medical Profile
Address Your residential address Profile Information
Occupation Your current profession or job title Health Context
Marital Status Your current marital status Profile Information

In addition to profile data, HealthArc may also collect health metric data generated by your device sensors, including step count, heart rate readings, sleep duration data, blood pressure logs entered by you, and blood oxygen (SpO₂) readings.

03 — How We Collect

How Your Data Is Collected

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Profile Setup: Information you directly enter when creating or updating your HealthArc profile (name, DOB, height, weight, blood group, address, occupation, marital status, gender).
  • Device Sensors: Health data collected passively through your Android device's built-in sensors, including the accelerometer (steps) and compatible health sensors.
  • Manual Entry: Health readings you manually record in the app, such as blood pressure values.
  • Authentication: Your phone number used for OTP (one-time password) verification to secure your account.
04 — How We Use Your Data

Purposes of Data Processing

The personal information and health data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes within the HealthArc application:

  • To create and maintain your personal health profile within the application.
  • To provide personalized health monitoring, insights, and tracking based on your specific profile attributes.
  • To calculate health metrics such as BMI using your height and weight data.
  • To calibrate health norms and thresholds appropriately based on your age, gender, and other profile data.
  • To enable the AI Assistant to provide contextually relevant health guidance based on your profile.
  • To authenticate your identity securely via OTP verification.
  • To display your health history and trends within the application.

We do not use your personal data for advertising, we do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not use your data for any purpose beyond the core health monitoring features of the application.

05 — Data Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data

Your health data and personal information are stored locally on your device. HealthArc is designed to keep your sensitive health information within your device's storage to maintain maximum privacy and control.

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • OTP-based authentication to ensure only you can access your account and health data.
  • Local storage of sensitive health data to minimize exposure to external networks.
  • No unnecessary transmission of personal data over the internet.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to maintain the physical security of your device.

06 — Data Sharing

How We Share Your Data

HealthArc does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to any outside parties. We do not share your personal health data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Your data may only be disclosed in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • If required to do so by applicable law, a court order, or by government or law enforcement authorities.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of HealthArc, its developers, users, or others.
07 — Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

Your profile data and health records are retained within the application for as long as you choose to keep the HealthArc application installed on your device. Your data is stored locally and is under your direct control.

You may delete your profile data at any time through the application's profile settings. Uninstalling the application from your device will permanently remove all locally stored data associated with your account.

08 — Your Rights

Your Data Rights

As a user of HealthArc, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all personal information stored in your profile at any time through the Profile Page in the application.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update or correct any inaccurate personal information through the profile settings.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete your profile data and health records at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Since data is stored locally on your device, you have direct access to your information at all times.
  • Right to Object: You may contact us if you have objections to how your data is being used within the application.

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

09 — Children's Privacy

Use by Minors

HealthArc is intended for general use and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Users under the age of 18 are encouraged to use the application under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

10 — Policy Changes

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the application or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the HealthArc application after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
